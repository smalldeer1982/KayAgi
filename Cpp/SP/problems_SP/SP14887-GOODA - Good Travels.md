# GOODA - Good Travels

## 题目描述

\\smallskip

## 输入格式

First line: 4 integers, $ N $ , $ M $ , $ S $ , and $ E $

Next $ N $ lines: 1 integer, $ f_i $ , for $ i = 1..N $

Next $ M $ lines: 2 integers, $ a_i $ and $ b_i $ , for $ i = 1..M $

## 输出格式

1 integer, the maximal amount of fun The Team can have on their trip.

## 样例 #1

### 输入

```
5 6 1 4
5
4
5
10
2
1 2
1 3
2 4
3 4
4 5
5 4
```

### 输出

```
22
```

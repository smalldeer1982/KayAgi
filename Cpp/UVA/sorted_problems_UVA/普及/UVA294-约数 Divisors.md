## 样例 #1

### 输入

```
3
1 10
1000 1000
999999900 1000000000
```

### 输出

```
Between 1 and 10, 6 has a maximum of 4 divisors.
Between 1000 and 1000, 1000 has a maximum of 16 divisors.
Between 999999900 and 1000000000, 999999924 has a maximum of 192 divisors.
```
